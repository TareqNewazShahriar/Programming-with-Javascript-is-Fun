Problem
A man has 49 cows. The first cow gives 1 KG of milk, the second cow gives 2 KGs of milk, and in this way the 49th cow gives 49 KGs of milk. The man has 7 sons. After his death, the 49 cows have to be divided among his 7 sons in such a way that all of them will get the equal number of cows and everyones' amount of milk will be same.
(This problem can be solved for any number of sons.)
