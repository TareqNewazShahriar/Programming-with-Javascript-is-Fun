Money Doubling Riddle
Think about a pond where, when someone dives to any corner of it with some money and comes up, his/her money becomes doubled. You are told to dive to the corners of the pond with some money and to put a specific amount of money in that corner. Then dive to the 2nd corner, comes up, and put the same amount of money you put on the 1st corner. Dive to every corner of the pond in the same way, come up and put the same amount in every corner. Continue until you dive to all four corners.
Now you have to find out that, how much to start with and how much money to put each time so that you have no money left at the end.

Hints:
1. The amount of money that to put, should be above zero.
2. Fraction is acceptable too in case of the starting money or the money to put.
